# Game Ideas

## Core Idea

Players become stronger by improving their own skills rather than only increasing character levels.

---

## Combat

- Skill-based sword combat
- Dodge
- Parry
- Counter
- Stamina
- Directional attacks
- Feints

---

## Enemy Design

Every enemy teaches one combat skill.

Goblin
- Timing

Wolf
- Positioning

Knight
- Parry

Assassin
- Reaction

Mini Boss
- Strategy

Boss
- Combination of all mechanics

---

## World Ideas

- Hidden Locations

- Dynamic Events
- Player Discoveries
